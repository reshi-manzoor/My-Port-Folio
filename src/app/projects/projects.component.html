Featured Projects

EConduct

This project is designed to maintain student profiles, including their scorecards, exam forms, admit cards, and datesheets. It integrates with third-party APIs for online payments like Razorpay and BillDesk, and is synchronized with around 150 colleges across the valley.

ECertification

This project focuses on maintaining certificates and degrees, mainly for postgraduate students, MPhil, and PhD candidates. It facilitates the sending of degree completion certificates to students, all concerned departments, media, the National Skill Repository, and coordinators.

Evaluation Tracking System

This system is designed to track the evaluation process of examination papers. It includes features for profiling the list of evaluators and tracking the papers assigned to them for checking.

Examination Status Tracking System

This system tracks examinations, including the creation of datesheets, results, and verification of students from the date of registration to the completion of their degree.

Xerox and Revaluation Modules (EResult)

These modules are designed for students to apply for photocopies (xerox) of their written examination papers and for the revaluation of backlog papers. The system includes features for applying and filling in the details for either process, and integrates a payment gateway for transactions.
